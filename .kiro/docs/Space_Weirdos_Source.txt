# Building a Space Weirdos Warband #

## Warband Building Rules ##

A warband of Space Weirdos consists of a Leader and troopers. 

Each warband has a Name, an Ability, Max Point Cost, and a Total Point Cost.

Warband total point values can be either up to 75 or up to 125 points.


## Space Weirdo Building Rules ##

Each trooper in the warband has a Name, Total Point Cost, Attributes, Weapons, Equipment, Psychic Powers, and Notes.

The leader has all the elements of a trooper and also has a leader trait.

One weirdo in the warband may cost up to 25 points. This is usually the Leader, but does not need to be. 

Each trooper may cost up to 20 points.

### Leader Traits ###

Optionally choose one of the following traits for the leader. 

Trait | Notes
Bounty Hunter | Once per round, when a weirdo from your warband is touching a down or staggered enemy, it can take a Use Item action to make the enemy weirdo out of action.
Healer | During the Initiative Phase, one of your weirdos within one stick of your leader may make a free Stand or Recover action with +1DT.
Majestic | Any time one of your warband has to make a Willpower roll, that weirdo may use the Leader’s Willpower instead.
Monstrous | Non-Leader weirdos must win a Willpower roll vs. your leader to move into contact.
Political Officer | During the Initiative Phase, before rolling, take one of your Warband within LOS of your leader out of action to make all other weirdos in the Warband ready, remove the broken condition from your warband, and gain +1DT to this Initiative roll.
Sorcerer | Psychic Powers actions cost 1 action instead of 2, but may still only use 1 per turn.
Tactician | +1DT to Initiative rolls.

### Warband Ability ###

Choose one of the following abilities for the Warband. The effect applies to all weirdos in the Warband. If the trait has a Rule, it will modify the associated warband building rule.

Trait | Notes | Rule
Cyborgs | Cyborg Equipment | All members of the Warband can purchase 1 additional equipment option.
Fanatics | Roll Willpower with +1DT for all rolls except Psychic Powers. | None
Living Weapons | Unarmed attacks do not have -1DT to Prowess rolls. | None
Heavily Armed | Heavily Armed | All Ranged weapons are 1 less Points Cost.
Mutants | Mutant | Speed, Claws & Teeth, Horrible Claws & Teeth, and Whip/Tail cost 1 less Points Cost.
Soldiers | Soldier | Grenades, Heavy Armor, and Medkits may be selected at 0 Points Cost.
Undead | Undead a second staggered condition does not take weirdos in this Warband out of action. | None
